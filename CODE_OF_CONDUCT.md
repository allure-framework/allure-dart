# Code of Conduct

Allure Dart follows the Allure Framework community standards. We want this
project to be a welcoming, professional, and practical space for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, especially when discussing bugs or design tradeoffs.
- Keep feedback focused on the work and its impact.
- Help newcomers understand the project context.
- Respect maintainers' time and decisions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Insults, intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam or unrelated promotion.
- Any behavior that makes the project unsafe or hostile for others.

## Reporting

If you see unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers through the
repository owner channels or by opening a private security advisory if the
report includes sensitive information.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other
reasonable action to protect the community.
